# AutoStream Company Policies

## Refund Policy
- No refunds are issued after 7 days from the date of purchase.
- Refund requests within the first 7 days are eligible for a full refund, no questions asked.
- Partial refunds are not offered for mid-cycle cancellations.
- To request a refund within 7 days, contact support@autostream.io with your order ID.

## Support Policy
- **Basic Plan**: Email support available Monday to Friday, 9am–6pm EST.
- **Pro Plan**: 24/7 dedicated support via live chat, email, and phone.
- Response time for Basic plan: within 24 hours on business days.
- Response time for Pro plan: within 1 hour, any time.

## Cancellation Policy
- You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard.
- After cancellation, your account remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.
- No data is deleted immediately upon cancellation — you have 30 days to export your content.

## Data & Privacy
- AutoStream does not sell user data to third parties.
- All videos are stored with AES-256 encryption.
- GDPR and CCPA compliant.

## Fair Use Policy
- Basic Plan accounts are limited to 10 video exports per calendar month.
- Attempting to circumvent limits via multiple accounts will result in account suspension.
- Pro Plan has no video limit but prohibits automated bulk API abuse.

## Upgrade & Downgrade
- You can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time; the price difference is prorated.
- Downgrading from Pro to Basic takes effect at the next billing cycle.

## Free Trial
- AutoStream offers a 14-day free trial on the Pro Plan for new users.
- No credit card required to start the trial.
- Trial accounts have full Pro features.
