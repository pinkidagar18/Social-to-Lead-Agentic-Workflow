# AutoStream Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

**What is AutoStream?**
AutoStream is an AI-powered SaaS platform that automates video editing for content creators. Upload your raw footage and AutoStream handles cutting, trimming, captioning, and export — in minutes, not hours.

**What platforms does AutoStream support?**
AutoStream is optimized for creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Facebook. We support all major export aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:5).

**Do I need video editing experience?**
No. AutoStream is designed for creators of all skill levels. The AI handles the technical work automatically.

## Technical Questions

**What video formats can I upload?**
AutoStream accepts MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM files up to 10GB per video.

**How long does processing take?**
Basic Plan: 5–15 minutes per video depending on length.
Pro Plan: Priority queue — usually under 3 minutes with 4K output.

**Can I use AutoStream on mobile?**
Yes. AutoStream has a web app optimized for mobile browsers, and native iOS and Android apps are coming in Q3 2025.

**Is there an API?**
Yes, API access is available on the Pro Plan. Documentation at docs.autostream.io.

## Billing Questions

**Can I switch plans mid-month?**
Yes. Upgrading is instant and prorated. Downgrading takes effect at the next cycle.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.

**Is there an annual discount?**
Yes — pay annually and save 20%: Basic at $23/month, Pro at $63/month.
